Disaster Survivor - All-in-One App for Disaster Preparedness and Recovery

Disaster Survivor is a mobile application designed to provide crucial assistance to individuals impacted by natural disasters. With a mission to empower communities in times of crisis. We understand the immense stress and uncertainty that natural disasters bring. Disaster Survivor aims to empower individuals and communities by offering a comprehensive suite of features accessible at your fingertips. Whether you're facing a hurricane, earthquake, or flood, our app equips you with the knowledge and resources needed to navigate challenging situations.


Key features

  • Real-Time Satellite Views:

    Gain valuable insights with access to real-time satellite imagery. Stay informed about weather patterns, potential flooding risks, and critical evacuation zones. This crucial information allows you to make informed decisions for your safety and the safety of your loved ones.

  • Flood Survival Guide:

    Knowledge is power, especially during a flood. Our app provides a comprehensive Flood Survival Guide. This easy-to-follow guide outlines essential steps for preparing for and surviving floods. Find clear instructions on evacuation procedures, safety measures to take within your home, and crucial tips to protect yourself and your belongings.

  • In-App Donations:

    In the aftermath of a disaster, every bit of support counts. Disaster Survivor facilitates secure and convenient in-app donations to verified disaster relief organizations. Contribute directly to the recovery efforts and help communities rebuild in the wake of devastation.

  • Global Disaster News:

    Stay informed about natural disasters unfolding around the world with our global disaster news feed. This feature allows you to be prepared to offer support to those in need, and gain valuable insights that might be relevant to potential threats in your own area.

  • Medical Help for Injuries:

    Natural disasters can often lead to unforeseen injuries. Our app provides information on how to treat common injuries sustained during disasters. Find clear first-aid instructions and access to a directory of medical resources to ensure you receive proper care.

  • Emergency Hotlines:

    When seconds matter, having access to emergency services is crucial. Disaster Survivor provides a comprehensive list of emergency hotlines for critical services like fire departments, police, and ambulances. This readily available information ensures you can get help quickly in case of an emergency.
