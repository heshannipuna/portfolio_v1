Skin Consultation Managing App - Java / OOP

This project encapsulates the OOP principles while addressing a practical real-world scenario: facilitating skin consultations seamlessly through a user-friendly application.

Within the Skin Consultation App, users can navigate scheduling and managing appointments with dermatologists. users can register, book appointments with preferred doctors, and even cancel appointments if necessary.
