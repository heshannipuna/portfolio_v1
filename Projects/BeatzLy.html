BeatzLy - Web based Music Streaming App.

BeatzLy is a simple dynamic web-based music streaming service that caters to music lovers' diverse tastes and preferences, akin to the popular platform Spotify.
It has features like shuffle, play next and previous songs, sound controls, and a equalizer.
